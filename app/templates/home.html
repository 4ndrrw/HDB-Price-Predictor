What Influences HDB Resale Prices?

Key variables that consistently shape property values across Singapore.

Location & Accessibility

Town popularity, MRT distance, nearby amenities and school clusters strongly influence valuation and buyer demand.

Floor Area

Larger units attract broader demographics, while compact units tend to see higher rental yields and liquidity.

Remaining Lease

Lease decay affects financing options and long-term value — flats with >70 years remaining maintain stronger price stability.

Storey Range

Low floors trade at a discount; mid floors offer balance; high floors command premiums for ventilation and views.

Flat Type & Configuration

The internal layout, upgrade potential and typical buyer group all contribute to the valuation trend within each flat type.

Neighbourhood Trends

Supply, demand, renewal projects and surrounding development create micro-trends that influence resale behaviour.

Basic vs Precise — Which Should You Use?

Both models are trained, but used differently depending on your goal.

Basic Mode

Fast, generalised, and input-light.

  • • Uses town + flat type + area + lease
  • • Great for quick estimates
  • • Similar accuracy to property portals
  • • Ideal when you don't know the exact address yet

Precise Mode

Fine-grained and location-specific.

  • • Uses full address + geolocation + storey
  • • Includes latitude/longitude spatial features
  • • Learns from price behaviour along specific streets
  • • Best for serious planning or budgeting
